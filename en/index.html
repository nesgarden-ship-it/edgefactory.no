Deal Readiness: Evidence Pack for sell-side (M&A)

Language: Norsk

Reduce uncertainty before DD. Deliver a verifiable decision trail documenting critical assumptions to reduce valuation friction and haircuts.

The delivery

A structured Evidence Pack that ensures traceability for transaction-critical decisions.

Strategic preparation before exit, carve-out, or sell-side M&A.

Presenting a ready decision trail at dataroom opening lets the seller control the narrative, reduces buyer uncertainty, and shortens the DD timeline. This is a scoped delivery, not a full project.

  • Evidence Cards: Verifiable documentation of key decisions.
  • Decision Ledger: Consolidated history and audit trail.
  • Fast delivery: Executed without system replacement (based on existing exports/data).
