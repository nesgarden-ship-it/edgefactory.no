Edge Factory is my initiative to address the biggest problem I see in the market right now: The gap between AI in theory and AI in production.

What I'm exploring

Many organizations hesitate to deploy AI in production because they lack control. I do this because I see how quickly critical logic disappears when AI is adopted without the right framework.

My hypothesis is that the problem isn't the models — it's how we govern and constrain them. I work with architecture that combines:

  • Deterministic logic: Rules that provide predictability, repeatability, and control.
  • AI as a tool: Only for interpretation, never as an uncritical source of truth.
  • Full traceability: An auditable trail from input → decision → output.
